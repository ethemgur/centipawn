# Privacy Policy for Centipawn

**Effective date:** May 16, 2026
**Last updated:** May 16, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Centipawn mobile application ("Centipawn", "the app", "we", "us") handles information when you use it. By using Centipawn you agree to the terms of this policy.

## Summary

Centipawn is a chess study and analysis app. It does **not** collect, sell, share, or transmit personal data for analytics, advertising, or profiling purposes. Chess games, analyses, and app preferences are stored **locally on your device**.

The only personal information the app handles is the basic Google account identifier you provide when you choose to sign in, and only for the purpose of authenticating you.

## Information we handle

### 1. Account information (only if you choose to sign in)

Centipawn offers optional account creation and Google Sign-In **solely for authentication**. When you sign in with Google, we receive a limited set of profile information from Google (typically your name, email address, profile picture, and a unique user identifier). This information is used only to:

- Verify your identity so you can sign in.
- Associate the session with your account.

We do **not** use this information for marketing, advertising, profiling, or any purpose other than authentication. We do not sell or share this information with third parties.

### 2. Locally stored data

The following are stored **only on your device** (in a local SQLite database) and are not transmitted to us:

- Chess games you import or create (PGN data and metadata such as event, players, date, result).
- Engine analyses and move evaluations generated locally on your device.
- Tags, review status, and similar app state.
- App preferences.

You can delete this data at any time by clearing the app's storage or uninstalling the app.

### 3. Information we do **not** collect

We do not collect:

- Analytics or usage telemetry.
- Crash reports automatically.
- Advertising identifiers.
- Location data.
- Contacts, photos, microphone, or camera input.

## Third-party services

- **Google Sign-In / Firebase Authentication** (only if you sign in): handled by Google. Your use is subject to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).
- **Stockfish chess engine**: runs locally on your device. It does not transmit any data.

## Children's privacy

Centipawn is not directed to children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

## Data security

Authentication is handled by Google's authentication services, which use industry-standard transport security (TLS). Locally stored game data is protected by your device's standard sandboxing and access controls.

## Your rights

Because Centipawn does not collect personal data beyond authentication identifiers, there is generally no personal data of yours that we store on our servers. You can:

- Sign out of the app at any time.
- Delete your local data by clearing app storage or uninstalling.
- Request deletion of any account-linked identifier by contacting us at the address below.

Depending on your jurisdiction (e.g., GDPR, CCPA), you may have additional rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your personal information. To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.
