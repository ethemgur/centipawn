# Data Privacy & Account Deletion

**Last updated:** May 16, 2026

## Requesting account and data deletion

To request the deletion of your account and all associated data, please send an email to **the64thsquare.dev@gmail.com** with the subject line **"Account Deletion Request"**. Please send the email from the address associated with your account so we can verify your identity.

## What gets deleted

Upon a verified request, we will delete:

- Your authentication record (Google Sign-In identifier, email, and any associated profile information held for the purpose of authenticating you).
- Any account-linked data stored on our side.

## What is stored only on your device

Centipawn stores your chess games, analyses, tags, and app preferences **locally on your device** in a SQLite database. This data is not held on our servers and is not part of a server-side deletion request. You can remove it at any time by clearing the app's storage or uninstalling the app.

## Timeline

We aim to process verified deletion requests within 30 days of receipt.
